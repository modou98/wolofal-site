Title: Ma jug feddali’g tuub akub cant man
Excerpt: Ma jug feddali’g tuub akub cant man - Ku for dog bu say kem forul sant kay
Theme: Diggante Sëriñ ak Taalibéem (Relation entre le taalibe et son Sëriñ)
---
Ma jug feddali’g tuub akub cant man
Ku for dog bu say kem forul sant kay

Gëram Yàlla ak sant Bàmbaa ma war
Amul kenn koo xamne ñee naa ko tay

Damaa dikk fi’g dex gu suuxal ñu rëy
Ma xuus ak sama’w nit ba far jàll wéy

Ludul dëkke cant ak ngëram jaaduwul
Ci man Baabakar mii ñu may ngën ji may

Samay rakk a ngii lab samay kem di lab
Samay mag di lab may am ug mucc céy

Damay sant xéewal gu rëy gii ma am
Nde kat sant xéewal du jur mukk ay

Ku am may gu rëy fàww cantam ga rëy
Ku cantam ga rëy ndollenam daaldi rëy

Bu ñiy deeka bon kay, ku deewul bonul
Bu taqoowul ak tuub ak cant, moy

Ludul cant gis naa ko muy moy gu rëy
Ta gis cant di’g may gu rëy nag ta doy

Lilee tax ma far bëgg a def cant kër
Ta far def ko mbubb ak mbalaan ak tubëy

Ta def ko’m ndox ak ñam ak ub sànt far
Aku’w fas ta war Marhaban, nii la kay
