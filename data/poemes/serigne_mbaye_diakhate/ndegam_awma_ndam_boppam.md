Title: Ndegam awma ndam boppam
Excerpt: Ndegam awma ndam boppam samab sang amna ndam - Ndegam manke naa moo xam samab sang mankiwul
---
Ndegam awma ndam boppam samab sang amna ndam
Ndegam manke naa moo xam samab sang mankiwul

Ndegam tuuti naa moo xam samab sang tuuti wul
Ndegam suufee naa moo xam samab sang suufee wul

Ndegam gàlli naa mo xam samab sang gàlli wul
Bu may sàgganit moo xam samab sang sàgganul

Ndegam jekkinaa moo xam samab sang jekkiwul
Ndegam fàttenaa moo xam samab sang fàttewul

Li lee tax lumay bon-bon ma xamni lu mën di xew
Damay mujj gën mbooloo mi, jël ndam ñu xëy ma fal

Ku war cib saxaaram tay nelaw it du tee mu raw
Waral ! Lépp loo bañ ñàkka war donga koy waral

Waral ci saxaarub cant dab ña la jiitu woon
Ta bul dof ba wàcc benn teerub tawat, wéyal !

Wéyal yaw ta bul teeral teeru budul ndigal
Ku teeral ci teerub moy ndigal, ñàkk ndam, dawal

Ku bëgg teraangaa’kub pal ak mbir mu jub yëwén
Na ngay teer akay teeral ci teerub muñ ak ndigal

Muñ ak ndigalay teerub ña gëm Yàlla, nég fa yaw
Saxaarub ngënéel ak bàrke loo mën a daj muñal

Saxaarub ngënéel ak bàrke buy ñëw nga daal di bég
Ta am darajaa’kuw tur wu siiw ak ndam ak ndawal

Sa jëf na rafet lool ak sa njort nga fas lu rëy
Di séenu ndam ak loo bëgg sab sang mànkiwul
