Xarit da lay xaral us lëf ciy yëfam ne la am
Koo xam ne kii du xaras lëf may la doo waayam
Seriñ da lay jariñ as lëf, yar la, yor la bu wér
Xamal la Yàlla, nga di’b santam, di ab jaamam
Baadoolo day bay di gor, may buuri ngar, di ko jey
Di wetti ñaq akay muñ sax ci ab toolam
Jëkkër dafay muñ akay muñlee ka sàmm ngoram
Ragal Boroomam te am njël, jiitu ag maasam
Jabar dafay yaru, tey muñ, neexi wax, bañ a wex
Rafet, rafet jikko, man boppam, ne cib néegam
Aw fas dafay gore, neexum ndañ, di gas aka tag
Waqam wa sew, wee wa réy, muy mingg ak ub taaram
As gor da fay gore, tey fonkiy waxam ba du deñ
Du fen, du jëw, te du sookeb jëw kii dib yaaram
Mbokkay ka bokk ak yaw xol, say wax it di waxam
Seeniy jef it bokk, muy sab caabi, ngay gaalam
Buuray ka xam Yàlla, sax cig topp, not bakkan am
Te doylu, doyle, amug baaxam, yorug leeram
Sàmmay ka sàmm iy céram, sàmm ub xolam ba du jëm
Cig moy, du jóg ci ndigal, bii sàmm a gën géeram
Jàmbur da fay jàpp ug njàmburam ci lu bon
Du def ludul noona, deeful dégg aw tooram
Sang ay ka sang i moroomam, tey jubal ak a jub
Nekkal di ab sang, yaw mii mbaate ngay waayam
